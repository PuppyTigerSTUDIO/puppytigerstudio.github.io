## What is Phase?

Phase is an app that allows you to record and manage your menstrual cycle.

**Key Features:**
- Easy period tracking
- Predicted period notifications
- Fertile window and ovulation day predictions
- Intuitive calendar view

---

## Key Features

### 1. Period Tracking

Easily record your period start and end dates.

### 2. Cycle Prediction

Predicts your next period based on recorded data.

### 3. Fertile Window/Ovulation Display

View your fertile window and predicted ovulation day on the calendar.

### 4. Notifications

Receive notifications before your predicted period.

---

## How to Use

### Step 1: Install the App

Search for **Phase** on Google Play Store or Apple App Store and install it.

### Step 2: Initial Setup

After launching the app, enter your last period start date and average cycle length.

### Step 3: Record

When your period starts, tap the date on the calendar to record it.

### Step 4: Check Predictions

View your next predicted period, fertile window, and ovulation day on the home screen.

## FAQ

### Q1: Where is my data stored?

**A:** All data is stored only on your device. It is not transmitted to external servers.

### Q2: Can I use this app if my cycle is irregular?

**A:** Yes, the prediction accuracy improves as you accumulate more records.

### Q3: How do I set up notifications?

**A:** You can adjust the notification time and days in the settings menu.
