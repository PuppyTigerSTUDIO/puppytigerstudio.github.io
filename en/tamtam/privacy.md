# TamTam Privacy Policy

**English** | [한국어](../../ko/tamtam/privacy.md)

**Last Updated: 2026-01-30**

---

## Introduction

PuppyTigerStudio ("we") is committed to respecting and protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is processed when using our mobile application.

---

## Information We Collect

**TamTam does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.**

This app operates entirely on your device and does not:
- Collect personally identifiable information
- Track your location
- Access your contacts or messages
- Store data on external servers
- Share information with third parties

The timer data and theme settings you configure are stored only on your device.

---

## Permissions We Use

TamTam uses the following permissions:

- **Notifications**: To display timer progress and alarm alerts
- **Vibration**: To provide vibration alerts when the timer completes
- **Wake Lock**: To keep the screen on while the timer is running
- **Background Execution**: To keep the timer running when the app is in the background

All data processed through these permissions is stored only on your device. No data is transmitted to external servers.

---

## Third-Party Services

TamTam uses the following third-party services:

- **Google AdMob**: For displaying advertisements. AdMob may collect device information for ad personalization. See [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).
- **Firebase Crashlytics**: For crash reporting to improve app stability. Crash reports may include device information but no personal data. See [Firebase Privacy Policy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy).

---

## Data Security

Since we do not collect or store any personal data, there is no risk of your personal information being compromised through this app.

---

## Children's Privacy

TamTam does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age.
